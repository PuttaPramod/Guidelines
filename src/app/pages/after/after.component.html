🌟 IIIT Courses After 10th Class 2025 🌟

Program Overview

IIITs offer a 6-year Integrated B.Tech program after 10th class— 2 years Pre-University Course (PUC) + 4 years B.Tech degree.

Program Structure

  • 📘 Years 1–2: PUC (11th–12th equivalent with MPC + BiPC tracks)
  • 💻 Years 3–6: B.Tech Engineering with specialized branches

Available Branches

  • 💻 Computer Science & Engineering
  • 🤖 CSE with AI & Machine Learning Specialization
  • 📡 Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • 🔌 Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • 🏗 Civil Engineering
  • ⚙ Mechanical Engineering
  • 🧪 Chemical Engineering (Nuzvid & RK Valley only)
  • 🔬 Metallurgical & Materials Engineering (Nuzvid & RK Valley only)

RGUKT Campus Locations

  • 📍 Nuzvid (Krishna District, AP) - 1,100 seats
  • 📍 RK Valley (Idupulapaya) (Kadapa District, AP) - 1,100 seats
  • 📍 Ongole (Prakasam District, AP) - 1,100 seats
  • 📍 Srikakulam (Srikakulam District, AP) - 1,100 seats
  • 📍 Basara (Nirmal District, Telangana) - Separate admission

Eligibility Criteria 2025

  • ✔ Academic: Pass SSC/10th (2025 batch) from AP/Telangana/CBSE/ICSE
  • ✔ Age Limit: ≤ 18 years (General), ≤ 21 years (SC/ST) as of Dec 31, 2025
  • ✔ First Attempt: Regular students with first attempt in SSC
  • ✔ Seat Distribution: 85% AP locals, 15% AP+Telangana open, 25% other states
  • ✔ Special Provision: 4% deprivation score for government school students

Selection & Merit Calculation

  • 📊 Primary: Total SSC/10th class marks & subject-wise performance
  • 🏫 Deprivation Score: +4% (4 marks per subject) for govt. school students
  • 🥇 Tie-breaker: Higher marks in Math → Science → English → Social
  • 🎯 Special Categories: NCC, Sports, CAP, PH, BSG reservations available
