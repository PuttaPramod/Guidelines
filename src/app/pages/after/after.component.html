🌟 IIIT Courses After 10th Class 🌟

Program Overview

IIITs offer a 6-year Integrated B.Tech program after 10th class— 2 years Pre-University + 4 years B.Tech degree.

Program Structure

  • 📘 Years 1–2: PUC (11th–12th equivalent)
  • 💻 Years 3–6: B.Tech Engineering

Available Branches

  • 💻 Computer Science & Engineering
  • 📡 Electronics & Communication
  • 🏗 Civil Engineering
  • ⚙ Mechanical Engineering
  • 🧪 Chemical Engineering
  • 🔌 Electrical & Electronics
  • 🔬 Materials Science & Metallurgy

Top RGUKT Campuses

  • 📍 Basar (Telangana)
  • 📍 Nuzvid (Andhra Pradesh)
  • 📍 Ongole (Andhra Pradesh)
  • 📍 RK Valley (AP)
  • 📍 Srikakulam (AP)

Eligibility Criteria

  • ✔ Pass 10th with 45–50% aggregate
  • ✔ Age ≤ 18 years (21 for SC/ST)
  • ✔ Regular first attempt students
  • ✔ 85% seats AP/Telangana, 15% open
